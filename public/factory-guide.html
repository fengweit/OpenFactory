合作流程 How it works

1
填写入驻申请
Submit your factory application
访问 openfactory.com/onboard 或扫描下方二维码。填写工厂名称、产品类别、MOQ、认证资质等基本信息。约5分钟完成。
Visit onboard page or scan QR code. Fill in factory name, product categories, MOQ, certifications. Takes ~5 minutes.
2
平台审核认证
Platform verification (1–3 business days)
OpenFactory 团队将通过微信联系您，确认工厂信息、生产能力和资质。通过后获得 ✓ 认证标识，优先展示给买家。
Our team contacts you via WeChat to verify factory info, capacity and certifications. Verified factories get priority placement.
3
收到询价通知
Receive quote requests via WeChat
当买家发送询价时，您会立即收到微信消息通知，包含产品详情和目标价。点击消息中的链接，无需登录即可直接回复报价。
When a buyer sends an RFQ, you get an instant WeChat message with product details. Tap the link to quote — no login required.
4
买家确认订单
Buyer places order — funds go into escrow
买家接受报价后，订单确认，货款由 OpenFactory 托管（非由买家直接付款）。您可以在工厂门户查看订单详情，更新生产进度。
Once buyer accepts, order is confirmed and full payment is held in escrow by OpenFactory. Update production milestones in the factory portal.
5
发货后资金释放
Ship goods → funds released to you
标记发货后，买家确认收货，款项自动释放到您的账户。全程资金有保障，不会出现跑单或拖款。
After shipment and buyer confirmation, funds are automatically released. No ghosting, no delayed payments.

常见问题 FAQ

Q: 加入 OpenFactory 需要费用吗？
Is there a fee to join OpenFactory?
入驻完全免费。平台仅在成功成交时收取小额服务费（约2-3%）。没有月费，没有隐藏费用。
Joining is free. Platform takes a small commission (~2-3%) only on completed transactions. No monthly fees.
Q: 买家都是哪些？
Who are the buyers?
主要为欧美、东南亚的中小型科技公司、硬件创业团队，以及使用 AI 工具自动采购的技术团队。订单规模通常为500-50,000件。
Primarily small-medium tech companies and hardware startups from US/EU/Southeast Asia, including AI-powered procurement teams. Typical order size: 500–50,000 pcs.
Q: 询价没有回复会怎样？
What if I don't respond to an RFQ?
建议在4小时内回复，以提高成交率。长期不回复会影响工厂在平台上的排名。每个询价都可以选择拒绝，不影响评分。
Respond within 4 hours recommended for best conversion. You can always decline an RFQ without affecting your rating.
Q: 货款什么时候到账？
When do I receive payment?
买家确认收货后2个工作日内，款项打入您指定的银行账户或支付宝/微信支付。
Within 2 business days of buyer confirming receipt, funds transferred to your bank account or Alipay/WeChat Pay.
Q: 如果买家投诉质量问题怎么办？
What if a buyer complains about quality?
OpenFactory 提供仲裁服务。双方提交证据（照片、检测报告等），平台在7个工作日内给出裁决。工厂提前上传出厂检测报告可大幅降低风险。
OpenFactory arbitrates. Both parties submit evidence. Decision within 7 business days. Submit QC reports upfront to reduce risk.
