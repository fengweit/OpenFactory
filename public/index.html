How it works
From search to shipped order — fully automated.
Your AI agent handles the entire sourcing workflow with 4 API calls. No email. No WeChat. No trade shows.
1
🔍
search_factories()
Filter verified GBA factories by category, MOQ, price tier. Returns structured JSON. Every factory on-site verified.
2
⚡
get_instant_quote()
Sub-second binding quote from pricing rules. No human in the loop. Your agent gets price, lead time, MOQ in <100ms.
3
✅
place_order()
Confirm the order. Payment held in escrow — released only after delivery confirmation. One POST request.

📦

Real-time production status via API. Webhooks for milestone updates. Your agent stays informed automatically.
